What is AngularJS?

AngularJS is a client-side model-view-* framework

What makes AngularJS unique from other client-side frameworks?

  • Two-Way Data-binding
  • Dependency Injection
  • Directives
  • Filters
  • Testability

Objectives

  • Review AngularJS Basics
  • Deep Dive into Directives
  • Dive into Filters and Underscore.JS
  • Understand Unit Testing
  • End to End testing
